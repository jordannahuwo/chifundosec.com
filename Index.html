About Our School

Chifundo Inclusive Secondary School is a pioneering institution committed to providing quality education for all students, including those with special needs, since its establishment in 2005.

Our school stands out for its inclusive approach, ensuring that every student receives the support they need to thrive academically and socially. We celebrate diversity and foster an environment where all students can reach their full potential.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of educators, Chifundo Secondary has consistently produced outstanding academic results while maintaining a strong focus on character development and community engagement.
