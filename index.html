About Our School

Our Mission

Chifundo Inclusive Secondary School, established on 16th September 2024, is committed to providing quality education for all students, including those with special needs.

Inclusive Approach

We ensure every student receives the support they need to thrive academically and socially, celebrating diversity and fostering potential. Students with special needs are given a 100% bursary to ensure equal access to education.

Our Facilities

With state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated educators, we achieve outstanding academic results while focusing on character and community.
